    Colorworks

    The newest iteration of Colorworks was established between 2018 and 2019, bringing on myself as bassist/vocalist, Deno Durant on drums, and Ash Einhorn on keys. 2018 started with a bang, as we released our self-titled Colorworks EP, an indie rock music collection consisting of old members and new, and we won a battle of the bands playing music off that record. We've played together all over the PNW, including classic Seattle venues such as The Sunset Tavern, Barboza, Nectar Lounge, Tractor Tavern, The High Dive, and many others. We've since released 2 new EPs a single, and a full-length album titled Vuja De. Check out the live footage below from our show at Conor Byrne Pub in Ballard, WA!

    The Candyflowers

    What started as a casual song-writing project with a good friend (Jesse Quigley) during the pandemic, turned into a fiery, passion-filled musical endeavor that allowed us to showcase our writing and musical style, as well as give us a creative outlet during a less than sublime period of time. The Candyflowers "Mind Folly" EP is an indie rock collection that seeks to give listeners a bumpin' rock experience with melancholic lyrics that can be interpreted in whatever way fits the narrative of the listener. Check out our music on Spotify!
